## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: What makes Mangy Dog Coffee special?**
A: We source high-quality beans from ethical suppliers and roast in small batches to ensure freshness and optimal flavor in every cup.

**Q: Do you sell whole bean or ground coffee?**
A: We offer both! You can choose whole bean or select your preferred grind size at checkout.

**Q: How should I store my coffee?**
A: Store in a cool, dark place in an airtight container. Avoid refrigeration as it can introduce moisture.

### Subscription Questions

**Q: Can I pause my subscription?**
A: Yes! You can pause or skip deliveries anytime through your account or by contacting us.

**Q: Can I change my coffee selection?**
A: Absolutely. You can change your blend, roast level, or bag size anytime before your next shipment.

**Q: Is there a commitment?**
A: No long-term commitment. Cancel anytime with no fees.

### Product Questions

**Q: Do you have decaf options?**
A: Yes, we offer Swiss Water Process decaf in our medium roast.

**Q: What brewing methods do you recommend?**
A: It depends on the roast! Light roasts are great for pour-over, medium for drip, and dark for espresso. We're happy to help you find the perfect method.

**Q: Do you sell gift cards?**
A: Yes! Gift cards are available on our website in various amounts.

### Order Questions

**Q: What's your return policy?**
A: If you're not satisfied with your coffee, contact us within 30 days and we'll make it right.

**Q: How long does shipping take?**
A: Standard shipping is 3-5 business days. Free shipping on orders over $50.

**Q: Do you ship internationally?**
A: Currently we ship within the US. Contact us for international inquiries.
